Join the Revolution

After originally leading the craft revolution in South Africa by opening some of the first micro-breweries in our country, KZN slowly fell off the pace being set by the Cape Town & Gauteng movement. Now with established breweries starting to establish themselves on a national level and new breweries opening at a rapid rate, we are showing that we are a force to be reckoned with.

The Revival

KZN Craft Revolution is a movement dedicated to celebrating and supporting the vibrant craft beer scene in KwaZulu-Natal. From Durban to the Midlands, our breweries are crafting world-class beers with local character and global ambition.

We believe in community, quality, and the spirit of innovation. Whether you're a seasoned beer lover or just starting your journey, there's a place for you in the revolution.

Our Breweries

From established names to exciting new openings, these are the pioneers of the KZN craft beer scene.

1000 Hills Brewing Company

Est. 2009

Established in 2009 (as Porcupine Quill Brewing Company), the brewery forms part of 1000 Hills Chef School where Advanced Students learn the art of Craft Beer Brewing. This brings a unique approach to brewing – with flavour and food in mind. In true craft tradition, we prefer not to follow any particular style too closely but rather experiment with our own styles.

Brewer:Trevor Maarschalk Owner:Trevor Maarschalk Web:1000hillsbrewery.co.za Contact:031 777 1566 Address: 4 Wootton Ave, Botha's Hill, KZN, 3660
🍺 Pale Ale 🏫 Chef School 🌿 Craft

Jabula Brew Co.

Est. 2019

Born and bred in the "031", Jabula Brew Co was founded by maverick home brewer and events organiser Warren Eades. His passion lies in unifying people from different communities through entertainment and craft beer. Jabula's "fruitilicious" beers embody the soul of summer.

Brewers:Warren Eades & Marc Freeman Owners:Eades, Edwards & Freeman Web:jabulabrew.co.za Contact:061 998 8108 Facilities: Contract brewery
🍺 NEIPA 🌞 Summer 🎉 Events

Lions River Craft Brewery

Est. 2015

Irish born Ronald McClelland has had a lifelong love affair with beer. He started brewing as a hobby, and after locals demanded more, he imported a Speidel Braumeister from Germany. Now with a beer garden open Thursday–Sunday, visitors can enjoy tours, taps, and country platters.

Brewer:Ronald McClelland Owner:Ronald McClelland Web:lionsriverbrewery.co.za Contact:083 775 8549 Address: 72 Riverside Farm, Lidgetton, Midlands
🍺 German-style 🌳 Beer Garden 🏞️ Midlands

Nguni Brewing Co.

Est. 2016

Nguni Brewing Co. was founded by three partners: one brew master and two beer enthusiasts. The brand features the head of a Nguni cow — a symbol of the East Coast's heritage. Each Nguni cow bears unique markings, much like human fingerprints, embodying their passion for creating unique beer.

Brewers:Shaun Lavery & Jordan Relph Owners:Hulbert, Lavery & Hulbert Web:ngunibeer.co.za Contact:031 700 4525 Address: 6–8 Moss Road, Westmead
🍺 Organic 🐄 Heritage 🌿 Natural

That Brewing Company

Est. 2013

How did THAT happen? Sean Roberts predicted craft beer would wash upon Durban's shores and opened Unity Bar. After a chance meeting with home brewer Paul Ten Hoorn Boer, the two built a 300l brewery in a garage. Today they operate from The Foundry in Station Drive with a restaurant and taproom.

Brewer:Paul Ten Hoorn Boer Owner:Paul Ten Hoorn Boer Web:thatbrewingco.co.za Contact:031 303 2747 Address: Ground Floor, The Foundry, 43 Station Drive
